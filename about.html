About BellaBliss Studio

Where beauty meets perfection

Our Story

Founded in 2020, BellaBliss Studio started with a simple mission: to provide premium beauty products and professional services that make every woman feel beautiful and confident.

What began as a small studio in Kathmandu has grown into a trusted name in the beauty industry, serving thousands of satisfied customers across the country.

Today, we offer a wide range of high-quality beauty products and professional services, all delivered with the same passion and dedication that started it all.
